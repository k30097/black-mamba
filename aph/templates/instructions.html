- How to use APH -

STEP 1 - create a new account.
signup using an email and a password that will be encrypted and stored securely on your local machine or login.

STEP 2 - choose one of the tools.
head to the dashboard. you can choose one of the tools available or open all of them in different tabs.

NMAP:
enter an ip address in the format of '123.123.123.123' or 'fe80::1'.
(optional) - insert a number indicating the last port that will be part of the scan.
the port range will be '1-[chosen #]'. any extra characters or different formats will raise an exception.

SCRAPING:
enter a URL in the format of 'https://website.com/endpoint'.
any extra character or different formats will raise an exception.

DIRBUSTER:
enter a URL in the format of 'https://website.com'.
any extra character or different formats will raise an exception.
the wordlist currently in use includes the top 2000 most common directory names.
Wordlist: 'https://raw.githubusercontent.com/digination/dirbuster-ng/master/wordlists/common.txt'

the scan results will be visible on the account page once it's completed. (look for the flash message at the top of the scan page)

STEP 3 - view the results.
head to your account page.
there you will see the current account that is being used, the date it was created on, the total number of scans and attacks performed and their results.
